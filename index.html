What is a Spiritual Detective?

A spiritual detective is a person dedicated to investigate and protect people from paranormal activity and entities. They hunt the shadows in the shadows and put their lives in risky in order to do that. It’s a job that you don’t ask for, you’re born to do that.

Because of the kind of cases a spiritual detective deals with, in order to be one, you have born as an ‘esper’ a person with paranormal powers. Some are born with the various abilities but the most common is to be able to see spirits, poltergeists, and to feel the residual energy that they left behind when they act on this dimension.

Mizuki also known as “Zukki The Spiritual Detective” is one of these people and she have been training his powers since she was very young to help the people around the world with unsolved cases with unknown circumstances that the police are not able to solve. Some cases involve cults and organizations that works for some entities. They use the power of the entities to get influence and power in the material world, and eliminate who discover them. And now they are seeing Zukki as an enemy that must disappear.

Our goal

We are the team of espers that work with Zukki and our goal is to help to solve cases and destroy the cults and organizations that are using powers from another world to achieve control over population. In order to do that we need new espers, we can teach you how to defend yourself and how to use your paranormal powers. With your help we can gather new information and help people around the world that are suffering with these crimes. In order to do that you can send information through our site or social media.
